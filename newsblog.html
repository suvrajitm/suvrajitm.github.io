2020

03/24/2020 -  Our manuscript has been accepted by Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling, Frontiers in CryoEM Modeling, Maji et al. 2020, titled "Propagation of Conformational Coordinates Across Angular Space in Mapping the Continuum of States from Cryo-EM Data by Manifold Embedding. Maji et al. 2020".

2019

01/28/2019 -  "ManifoldEM" alpha version 0.1.0 released. It is a Python software package for obtaining a continuum of conformational states of molecular machines using cryo-EM data.(Reference: Dashti et al. 2014)



2017

10/04/2017 -  Jacques Dubochet, Joachim Frank and Richard Henderson wins the 2017 Nobel Prize in Chemistry.

02/22/2017, 04/07/2017 -  Dr. Joachim Frank wins the 2017 Wiley Prize.

02/15/2017 -  Our manuscript has been accepted by the Journal of Physical Chemistry B, titled "Quantitative Characterization of Domain Motions in Molecular Machine. Maji et al. 2017".
